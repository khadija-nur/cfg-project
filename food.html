Eating in Borneo

Borneo is a cultural melting point, the combination of influences from Malaysia, Southern China and India and indigenous (Dayak) makes it never boring to eat in Borneo. Borneo cuisine staples include fried rice, barbequed prawns and grilled fish, whilst the rainforest provides some local treats including rambaton and bambagan.
